Sadabahar Flower Name in English

The term Sadabahar is the Hindi name for a popular flowering plant called Periwinkle in English. The scientific name for this plant is Catharanthus roseus. It is known for its attractive and colourful flowers, which come in shades of pink, purple, and white.

Kanakambaram Flower in English Name

The flower known as Kanakambaram in Hindi is commonly referred to as Marigold in English. Marigolds belong to the genus Tagetes and are known for their vibrant and cheerful blooms. They come in various shades of yellow, orange, and maroon, and are often used in floral arrangements, garlands, and decorations.

Sampangi Flower in English Name

The flower known as Sampangi in Hindi is commonly called Magnolia in English. The scientific name for this flower is Magnolia champaca. It’s a fragrant flower native to Southeast Asia and is highly valued for its beautiful and aromatic blooms.

Sampangi or Magnolia is often used in perfumes, incense, and religious ceremonies. The flowers are usually yellow or orange in colour and have a unique and captivating fragrance.

Bijli Flower Name in English

The flower named Bijli in Hindi refers also to the English flower name Marigold. The difference is that Bijli flowers typically are white in colour.

Champa Flower in English Name

The Champa flower is commonly known as the Plumeria or Frangipani flower in English. Plumeria is a tropical flowering plant native to the Caribbean, Central America, and Asia. It is known for its beautiful and fragrant flowers that come in various colours, including white, yellow, pink, and red. The flowers are often used in making perfumes and religious offerings.

What is Mogra Flower in English?

The Hindi word Mogra is commonly known as Jasmine in English. Jasmine is a fragrant flowering plant that belongs to the genus Jasminum. It is known for its small, white, star-shaped flowers and sweet, intoxicating scent. Jasmine is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and its flowers are used in making perfumes, essential oils, and teas. The fragrance of jasmine is highly cherished, and it is often associated with love, beauty, and spirituality.

Bela Flower in English

Like Mogra, he flower Bela is also known as Jasmine in English. So, Bela is the Hindi name for the Jasmine flower.

Orchid Flower in Hindi

The orchid in Hindi is “ऑर्किड फूल” (pronounced as Orkid Phool). Orchids are native to various parts of the world and are found in different regions including tropical rainforests, temperate forests, and even deserts. Orchids are known to be particularly diverse and abundant in tropical regions such as Southeast Asia, Central and South America, and parts of Africa.

Samanthi Poo in English

Samanthi Poo is commonly known as Chrysanthemum in English. So, Samanthi Poo refers to the Chrysanthemum flower in Hindi.

Indian Flower Names

India is blessed with a rich floral diversity and a variety of native flowers that are integral to its botanical heritage. Here are some of the most popular Indian flowers and their English names:

  • Lotus (Nelumbo nucifera) – Kamal (कमल)
  • Jasmine (Jasminum sambac) – Mogra (मोगरा)
  • Marigold (Tagetes erecta) – Gainda (गेंदा)
  • Rose (Rosa) – Gulab (गुलाब)
  • Sunflower (Helianthus annuus) – Surajmukhi (सूरजमुखी)
  • Hibiscus (Hibiscus rosa-sinensis) – Gurhal (गुड़हल)
  • Orchid (Orchidaceae) – Orkaid (ऑर्किड)
  • Bougainvillea (Bougainvillea) – Booganvel (बूगनवेलिया)
  • Chrysanthemum (Chrysanthemum) – Shevanti (शेवंती)
  • Gulmohar/Flamboyant (Delonix regia) – Gulmohar (गुलमोहर)
  • Frangipani/Plumeria (Plumeria) – Champa (चम्पा)
  • Parijat/Night-flowering Jasmine (Nyctanthes arbor-tristis) – Parijat (पारिजात)
  • Tube rose (Polianthes tuberosa) – Rajnigandha (रजनीगंधा)
  • Indian Laburnum/Golden Shower (Cassia fistula) – Amaltas (अमलतास)
  • Gladiolus (Gladiolus) – Sword Lily (स्वॉर्ड लिली)

To depict a drawing of the Kaner ka phool, you can start by drawing a central circle as the base of the flower. From this circle, extend five curved lines outward, connecting them at the tips to form the petals. Make sure to give the petals a slightly wavy or ruffled look to add a natural touch. FAQ about Flower Names in English

You’ve got any questions? You might find your answer here!

What are the 5 flowers names in English?

The 5 most common English flowers names are: 1. Rose, 2. Lotus, 3. Marigold, 4. Jasmine, and 5. Sunflower.

How many types of flowers?

There are more than 400.000 types of flowers and flowering plant species in the world.

What is the name of jasmine flower in Hindi?

The name of the Jasmine flower in Hindi is आम चमेली and translates to “Mango Jasmine” in English. “आम” (aam) means “mango” and “चमेली” (chameli) refers to “jasmine.” It is a descriptive term used to describe a variety of jasmine flowers that have a scent reminiscent of mango.

What is the name of 10 flowers?

The names of ten flowers are: 1. Hibiscus, 2. Lavender, 3. Magnolia, 4. Rose, 5. Lily, 6. Oleander, 7. Daisy, 8. Tulip, 9. Dahlia, and 10. Bluestar flower.

What is the flower champa called in English?

Champa is called in English Plumeria or Frangipani and is the national flower of Laos, where it is known under the local name champa or dok champa.
